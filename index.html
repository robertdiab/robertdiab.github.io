I am a professor in the Faculty of Law at Thompson Rivers University in Kamloops, British Columbia.

I write about law and technology.

My writing has appeared in the Los Angeles Review of Books, Salon, and the National Interest, among other venues.

I have also written scholarship on human rights and civil liberties, internet governance, and digital privacy.
