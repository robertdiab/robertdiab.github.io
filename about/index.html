This site follows developments in law and policy on digital and network technology.

I’m a prof in the Faculty of Law at Thompson Rivers University in Kamloops, British Columbia.

My writing focuses on a range of tech-related issues, including most recently internet governance, compelled decryption, and digital privacy.

Topics I follow:

  • internet fuelled populism and the demise of authority

  • platform capitalism

  • surveillance capitalism

  • telecom policy

  • intellectual property

  • quantum computing

  • AI, machine learning, and law enforcement
