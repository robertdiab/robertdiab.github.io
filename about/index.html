About

I am a professor in the Faculty of Law at Thompson Rivers University in Kamloops, British Columbia.

I write here about developments in digital technology and their impact on our rights and the way we live.

I have written essays and scholarship on a range of topics in tech, including internet governance, compelled decryption, and digital privacy.

I have also written about national security and civil liberties (full list of pubs.)
