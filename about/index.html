About

2021-03-21

Hi, I’m Robert Diab.

I teach criminal law and evidence in the law school at Thompson Rivers University, in Kamloops BC, and practice law with Jensen Law.

I enjoy keeping up with new developments in tech and spending time with my wife and two children.

I have published papers on a few tech topics: compelled decryption, password compulsion, and global content takedown orders – along with a fair bit about national security.

I have also written about the impact of the net on writers, artists, and musicians.
