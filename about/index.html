About

I work in the Faculty of Law at Thompson Rivers University and with the criminal defence firm Jensen Law in Kamloops, BC.

I’ve written essays and articles on law and technology, civil liberties, and human rights.

I co-edit the Canadian Journal of Comparative and Contemporary Law, and hold a PhD in law from the University of British Columbia.

When not spending time with family or watching the Leafs, I’m on the ice trying not to fall.

Find me on Twitter @robertdiab.
