About

I am an associate professor in the Faculty of Law at Thompson Rivers University in Kamloops, British Columbia, with a background in human rights and civil liberties.

I have wide ranging interests in tech. Some of them can be found here in shorter essays and scholarship on topics that include internet governance, compelled decryption, and digital privacy.
